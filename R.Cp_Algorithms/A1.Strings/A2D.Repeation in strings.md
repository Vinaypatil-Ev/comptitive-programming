# Repetition in string

A **Repetition** of string is multiple occurrence of substring(continuous string) into strem of characters.

**Example**:

Consider the repetitions in the following example string:

acababaee

* ab occurred 2 times
* ba occurred 2 times
* e occurred 2 times

Another example:

aabaabaa

* aba occurred 2 times
* aa occurred 3 times
* ba occurred 2 times
